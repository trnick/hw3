Blockchain Simulation Bot 

Languages:
Python
JavaScript

I was part of a team of other Computer Science students in a locally held hackathon, Valley Hacks, with the theme of ducation and finance. We collectively decided to develop a Discord bot to simulate how blockchain functions on a high-level (using our own example cryptocurrency). We achieved second place by coordinating with a team of other Computer Science students to complete and present our project in three days. Experience collaborating with a team of unfamiliar programmers, knowledge about developing in Python, understanding of blockchain and it's high-level implementation.
